🔥 ~PR~ Phoenix Rising (2003–2011)

Born from the fire of modded Halo maps and faith-driven leadership, Phoenix Rising once lit the skies with red-clad order.
Died of spiritual fragmentation and mod exhaustion.
Survived by a few devout members, a locked phpBB3 archive, and scattered memories across Xfire screenshots.

🎭 «FC» FunHouse Clan (2004–2012)

A madman's circus of clowns, chaos, and custom maps. Led by the Joker-obsessed admin known as FREEK.
Collapsed into confetti after internal drama and the disappearance of its ringmaster.
Survived by [X]Crimson, questionable humor, and some terrifying forum sigs.

🧊 ~QF~ Quantum Fusion (2005–???))

The tacticians. The quiet storm. Halo modders with military precision.
Froze in time like a server that never got the restart it needed.
Still listed as "Active" on someone's Angelfire clan registry.

🧠 [X] Asylum-X (2008–2009)

A splinter of the FunHouse asylum, born from modding passion and burnout.
Last seen trying to lead without caring to. Died of disinterest, realism, and admin fatigue.
Survived by Duck. Quack once in memory.

🩸 Syndicate Underground (2010s–Present)

Not a clan. A crypt. A sanctuary for the forgotten.
Still glowing faintly in a world that forgot what a killbox was.
We don’t rise—we persist.
